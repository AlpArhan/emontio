Project Emontio

Emontio scans web, extracts articles, conducts linguistic and sentiment analysis for tone extraction. Project Emontio attempts to give a consultation on how the financial markets may react to news release.

Try the demo, fill in the blanks:

Enter the Keyword and the Stock Name of the company you want to analyze. Pick the webpage that you want to scan the articles of. Ex: CNN, WSJ, MarketWatch, Bloomberg and etc.




Note: All fields are case sensitive.
